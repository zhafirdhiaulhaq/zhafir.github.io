Get to Know Us,
Arunika: Committed to Excellence for You
We are a trading company in agriculture products. Our company gives you a new experience to explore Indonesian Agricultural Products with comprehensive and intensive consultation.
Under the auspices of PT. Agro Nusantara Sukmawijaya, Arunika aspires to be able to support agricultural development in Indonesia, by implementing a sustainable agricultural system as a whole.
Vision
Become an export brand of agricultural products that can make a direct contribution to farmers to support the sustainable agriculture concept.
Mission
  • Provide knowledge to farmers to increase product selling value and maintain production stability.
  • Carry out mutually beneficial sustainable cooperation with downstream parties to maintain the stability of farmer production.
  • Creating future farmer development programs by providing non-formal education for farming families about agricultural sustainability from an environmental and business perspective
